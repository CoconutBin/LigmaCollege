Notice for Clubs (5/Mar/2022)

Every Year, some clubs will be disbanded due to inactivity, breaking guidelines, or administrative reasons.


This Year, 5 Clubs will be disbanded in 2 weeks:

- The White Snake Club will be disbanded due to the end of the collaboration between the Church and us.

- The Modern Fashion Club will be disbanded due to breaking guidelines on sexual activity, violence, and use of drugs.

- The Romance Fortune Club will be disbanded due to breaking guidelines on language, as well as personal reasons from the administation.

- The Light Music branch of the Anime Club will be disbanded due to inactivity, and failure to make music.

- The Modern Literature Club will be disbanded due to inactivity

Any members of the Church of the White Snake, please report to the Main Office in the Religious Relic Mueseum.

Collaboration with the Church of the White Snake has Ended! (5/Mar/2022)

Any members of the Church of the White Snake, please report to the Main Office in the Religious Relic Mueseum.
