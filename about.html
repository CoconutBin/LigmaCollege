Who are we?

Ligma College is a College located in Almon, Soranaland. We strive for Unique People that have the potential to Inspire or create the next major inspiration

We give Scholarships to people from Any Background we see has potential!

From Mathmeticians, to Artists, and Even Minimum wage workers.

If you believe that you can change the world, or influence others to do so, go to our contact page and apply for a scolarship There.

If you want to study and educate youself here, but you don't think you have the potential, don't worry! We offer Tutoring, and you can always pay for the chance to learn here in full price. Go to the Shop tab for our Prices, and check out the Payment tab on what payment methods are possible here.


What makes us Different?

Ligma College Offers Traditional Schooling, and Experemental Classes called the 'Sugmanutz' Method.

the 'Sugmanutz' Method is an experimental type of class that educates students with the Experimental ai-based technology from Kanyu Inc.

the technology adapts to a student's learning pattern, and gives the results to the Department of Sugondez Nutz, which then gives the students the necessary Outside experience that they need to get inspired and innovate.

Students that have the Scholarship must learn a mix of Traditional and the 'Sugmanutz' Method, while students Paying for Admission get a choice between Entirely Learning with 1 Method, or Mixing Both Together.

For Students that paid for Tutoring, they'll be taught Traditionally, Unless they pay an added fee for the resources needed


Why is the "Sugmanutz" Method so Expensive?

The "Sugmanutz" Method is a New Technology, which is experimental with low Demand, We, at Ligma Coll, are Trying as much as possible to lower the price of the "Sugmanutz" Method, but currently, the cost for licensing the software is the biggest cost right now.

The Spring Welcome Ceremony is an Event that Occurs Every New Semester, it showcases the skill, inovativeness, and the potential that Students at our College have.

Students that Have Attended the School can Host Events, while Students that are New to the School can Enjoy those Events
